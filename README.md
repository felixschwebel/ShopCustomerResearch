# ShopCustomerResearch
A project to practice different Machine Learning Techniques and models on customer data of an imaginative shop.

<img width="504" alt="Screenshot 2023-05-25 at 16 54 57" src="https://github.com/felixschwebel/ShopCustomerResearch/assets/111788725/dddd1689-86ab-4485-bc70-568698a10170">

<br></br>
**Regression**
- Linear Regression, with *R-squared of 0.16*
- Polynomial Regression, with 8 degrees with *R-squared of 0.31*
- Support Vector Regression (SVR), with *R-squared of 0.34* 
- Decision Tree Regression, with *R-squared of 0.28* 
- Random Forest Regression, with *R-squared of 0.32*
<img width="1431" alt="Screenshot 2023-05-25 at 17 21 24" src="https://github.com/felixschwebel/ShopCustomerResearch/assets/111788725/efabb09e-aeea-4bc8-aacb-954776413881">


<br></br>
**Classification** 
- Logistic Regression, with an *accuracy-score of 76 %* 
- K-Nearest Neighbour (K-NN), with an *accuracy-score of 72 %* 
- Support Vector Machine (SVM), with an *accuracy-score of 82 %* 
- Naive Bayes, with an *accuracy-score of 78 %* 
- Decision Tree Classification, with an *accuracy-score of 80 %*  
- Random Forest Classification, with an *accuracy-score of 86 %*
